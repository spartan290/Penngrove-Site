Marin

Coastal-meets-town living with strong lifestyle appeal.

What Marin feels like

Marin is known for lifestyle. Many buyers are drawn to the blend of natural beauty and proximity to the city, with a strong emphasis on quality of life and community feel. Each town has its own personality, so “Marin” is really a collection of very different micro-markets.

Homes and property types

Options range from classic neighborhoods to more private settings depending on the location. Because Marin varies so much town to town, the best approach is to narrow the lifestyle first, then focus the search.
