Sonoma

Classic wine country character with a slower, intentional pace.

What Sonoma feels like

Sonoma is often chosen for its wine country identity and a pace that feels more relaxed and intentional. It can feel like a destination while still being a real place to live.

Homes and property types

There is variety, from in-town options to properties that feel more rural depending on the area. If you are choosing Sonoma, it helps to get clear on whether you want proximity to town life or more privacy and land.
