San Francisco

Neighborhood-driven city living with huge variety block to block.

What San Francisco feels like

San Francisco is all about neighborhoods. The lifestyle can change dramatically from one area to the next, and even from one street to the next. For buyers, the biggest leverage is choosing the right neighborhood for how you actually live.

Homes and property types

Housing varies widely, from condos to classic single family formats depending on the area. Because the city is so pocket-specific, a plan matters more than browsing. The right approach is to define priorities, then focus.
