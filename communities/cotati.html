Cotati

Compact, friendly, and easy to live in with a local-town core.

What Cotati feels like

Cotati has a small-town footprint with a simple, practical vibe. It is the kind of place where daily life can feel easy. For buyers who want to be in the middle of things without the noise of a larger city, it can be a strong fit.

Homes and property types

Expect a mix of established neighborhoods and homes that feel approachable. Some buyers choose Cotati for convenience and value relative to surrounding areas, while still staying close to the broader Sonoma County lifestyle.

Who it fits best

Cotati works well for people who want a straightforward home base, quick access to nearby towns, and a calm day-to-day routine.
