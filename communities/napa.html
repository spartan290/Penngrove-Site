Napa

Wine country lifestyle with a strong destination feel.

What Napa feels like

Napa has a recognizable wine country identity and tends to feel more “destination-driven” in many areas. It can be a great fit for buyers who want the wine country lifestyle front and center.

Homes and property types

Housing varies widely by area, from neighborhood living to properties that feel more private and estate-like. The key is being clear about the lifestyle you want, because Napa can feel very different depending on location.
