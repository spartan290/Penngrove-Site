Meet Aaron Loewenthal

Real estate is not simply about buying and selling property. At its core, it is about guidance, judgment, and having the right person in your corner when the stakes are meaningful. As the owner and broker of Penngrove Real Estate, Aaron Loewenthal has built his business around those ideals, offering clients a level of attention and strategic insight that is increasingly rare in today’s market.

Aaron is deeply rooted in the community he serves. He lives in Penngrove with his wife and their four children, and their family life is fully woven into the rhythm of the town. His wife is the owner of Hello Penngrove, a well-loved local business that has become part of the area’s character. Their decision to build both a life and a business here was intentional, reflecting a long-term commitment to the community rather than simply maintaining a professional presence.

With more than a decade of experience in real estate, Aaron brings a steady and informed approach to every transaction. His expertise extends beyond traditional residential sales into investment properties, land opportunities, and development potential. He understands how to evaluate a property not only for what it is today, but for what it can become, allowing clients to make smarter and more confident decisions.

Clients often remark on Aaron’s calm presence and thoughtful guidance throughout the process. He believes real estate should never feel chaotic or pressured. Instead, his role is to provide clarity, anticipate challenges before they surface, and negotiate with quiet strength on behalf of those he represents.

Penngrove Real Estate was founded with a deliberate vision — to create an independent brokerage where relationships come before volume and service is never transactional. Independence allows Aaron to tailor strategies to each client, remain deeply involved in every detail, and deliver a level of discretion that today’s buyers and sellers value.

For sellers, this means thoughtful positioning, strategic marketing, and negotiation designed to maximize both value and terms. For buyers, it means having an advisor who understands the nuances between neighborhoods, the long-term implications of a purchase, and how to compete intelligently in a dynamic market.

Aaron’s approach is simple: fewer clients, deeper service, better results. Above all, he believes great representation should feel steady and personal — the kind of partnership that continues well beyond a single transaction.

Rooted in Penngrove. Representing distinctive properties throughout Sonoma County.
